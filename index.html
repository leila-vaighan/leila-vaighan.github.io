About Me

I'm Leila, I recently graduated from Master of Science in Computer Engineering from Eastern Mediterranean University (EMU) under supervision of Prof.Dr.Önsen Toygar. Prior to that, I got my Bachelor’s degree from Allameh Dehkhoda University and my Associate degree from Pajohesh Sanaie Iran University.

My research area is specialized in machine learning, deep learning, and image processing.

My recent research a conference paper titled "Exploring Deep Learning Architectures for Multiple Apple Leaf Disease Classification," presented at the 3rd International Conference on Advanced Engineering, Technology and Applications in Catania University, Italy.

In addition to my research, I have worked as a research assistant in the Computer Engineering Department at EMU, I also have extensive teaching experience as a teaching assistant in laboratories of different courses.

My current research focuses on machine learning and deep learning methods, particularly in healthcare technologies and image classification.
